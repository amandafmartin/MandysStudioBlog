DIY Waterproof Mascara Remover


Dear reader,

Today, I am going to show you my all-time favorite eye makeup remover trick. I use waterproof mascara and a ton of eye primer because I have very watery eyes. I have a tendency to constantly rub them. By the end of the night, my eye shadow is typically gone and I am left looking like a raccoon! My mascara runs all over my under-eye area. I have tried almost every trick under the sun to remove my waterproof mascara and none of them have worked, except for this one. I have found that raw coconut oil removes it super quickly and effectively. Before I use the coconut oil, I grab a makeup wipe and wipe off most of my face makeup. Then, I rub about a teaspoon sized amount of the oil on my dry eyes. I rub in circular motions. Once I feel that all my mascara is broken down, I grab some of my facial cleanser and wash my face as I normally would. The coconut oil doesnâ€™t leave my face dry and it removes all of the makeup. Finally, I continue with my night-time skincare routine.
