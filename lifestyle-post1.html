Coconut Body Scrub


Dear reader,

Ingredients:
1-1½ tbs. virgin coconut oil {should be solid} (found in the organic section of grocery store or health food store)
¼ -½ cup of sugar (I like raw sugar because the granules are bigger which makes the scrub more abrasive)
1 glass bowl
Directions:

1) Scoop out coconut oil into the glass bowl.
2) With spoon, flatten the coconut oil. This makes it easier to mix the sugar with the oil.
3) Pour sugar onto the flattened coconut oil and begin to mix. This might take a few moments because the oil is a solid, which makes it harder to mix. Don’t worry though, the results are worth it.
4) Once fully mixed, the scrub is ready for use.
Results:
The scrub will leave your skin soft, moisturized, shiny, and smelling amazing!
